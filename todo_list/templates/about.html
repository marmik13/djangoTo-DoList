  • About TODO App
  • The TODO app lets a user create, read, update and delete to-do tasks. A task has a title and description and can be completed or active. Completed tasks can be deleted at once.
  • How to use?
  • It is simple to use this app, just write your to-do task in the text box and hit the Add To List button, that's it and Your task is added to list. You can edit your tasks by clicking on it. You can cross off your completed tasks and delete them from list.
